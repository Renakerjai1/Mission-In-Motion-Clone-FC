Bringing HOPE. Changing LIVES. Spreading the LOVE of CHRIST Across the Street. “Across the Street” Mission Opportunities are local mission opportunities here in the Tri-State area. Join us as we make a POSITIVE IMPACT and minister to the homeless, veterans, families with special needs, people of all ages, and at local disaster sites, etc. These are great opportunities to share the love of Christ and be the hands and feet of Jesus as HE leads. Opportunities varies according to the event. Experience the joy of serving and make a POSITIVE IMPACT in the lives of others! Check our "Home" page or Facebook for upcoming H.O.P.E. Opportunities or Email us for more info. If you have an idea please contact us we'd love to hear from you.

Let us know if you do not live in the Cincinnati area. We would be happy to help you plan a weekend or week long local Cincinnati/Dayton Mission Opportunity. Let us do the planning for you or your group!

Positive Impact Weekend

Join us for a weekend of impacting families and individuals across the Tri-State area. During this time, you will be able to make a POSITIVE IMPACT in the lives of others, by helping meet their PHYSICAL and SPIRITUAL needs. Opportunities MAY include painting, landscaping, organizing clothing and baby items, helping individuals with disabilities, serving lunch to those in need, as well as random acts of kindness, etc. Join us in this exciting "Across the Street" mission opportunity and be the hands and feet of Jesus.

A Night of H.O.P.E.

Bringing HOPE at NIGHT. Sharing CHRIST. Changing LIVES across the street. A Night of H.O.P.E brings individuals, families, and groups together to serve the needs of people across the street.

Shoe Packing Parties

Shoes for H.O.P.E. Shoe Packing Parties are great team building opportunities and great for individuals, families, and groups of all ages! Periodically, we have lots of shoes that need to be sorted, counted, and bagged and ready for shipment to those in need.

A Day of H.O.P.E. Homeless and Low Income Outreach September 12, 2020

A Day of H.O.P.E. is held annually to encourage, empower, and impact the homeless and low income community. This is an awesome outreach for individuals, families, churches, groups, businesses, schools, and organizations, to be involved in whether you live locally or anywhere! Will you help bring HOPE and be the hands and feet of Jesus?

Global Impact Party

Global Impact Parties help you be the hands and feet of Jesus behind the scenes. From time to time we have a variety of small projects that need to be done for one or more mission opportunities. Will you help behind the scenes? Contact us on how YOUR GROUP can HOST A PRIVATE Global Impact Party.
