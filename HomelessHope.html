What We are Searching For

We are looking for new or gently used shoes to give to those who are not fortunate enough to own a sturdy pair of shoes. All donated new and gently used shoes will be redistributed through micro-enterprise partners and used in developing nations for impoverished people to start, maintain, and grow an enterprise to feed, clothe, and house their families.

We provide the following free services at these events

  • Fresh hot cooked meal
  • Covered seating area
  • Sack lunch to take for later
  • Children's area
  • Health Screenings
  • Personal hygiene kits
  • School supplies
  • Haircuts
  • Clothing
  • Social/Referral Agencies
  • An encouragement tent

Ways you can help us

Be a Sponsor

A Day of H.O.P.E. Homeless and Low Income Outreach receives no governmental funds and depends solely on individual and corporate sponsorship. Your sponsorship will help assure the success of A Day of H.O.P.E. Homeless and Low Income Outreach. We rely solely on the donations of the community to make these events possible.

Be a Partner

A Partner is a church, organization, or individual(s) who participates by donating gift cards, products, resources, volunteers, or any type of In-Kind donation
