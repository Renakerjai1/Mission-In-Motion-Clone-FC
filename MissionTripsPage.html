Looking to Serve?

Join us on a Mission Trip!

Three Ways YOU Can Make a POSITIVE IMPACT:

  • PRAY for the H.O.P.E. Team, before, during, and after the mission trip
  • DONATE to help purchase and send supplies, help send the team, etc.
  • VOLUNTEER with us on one of our H.O.P.E. Mission Trip Teams

Bringing hope. Changing lives. Spreading the love of Christ AROUND the WORLD! Missions In Motion International Ministries "Around the World" mission trip opportunities are fun, exciting, and such a blessing!

They are a great way to make a POSITIVE IMPACT, both spiritually and physically, in the lives of others by being the "hands and feet of Jesus"!

We take the guesswork out of planning and will help you every step of the way. Our opportunities are very family friendly and cost effective. We excel at organizing, planning, and executing short term mission trips. Additionally, most of our trips are all-inclusive.
