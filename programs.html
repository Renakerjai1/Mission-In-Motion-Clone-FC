H.O.P.E. Programs

Our Programs are designed around H.O.P.E., which stands for "Helping Other People Excel." We have some AMAZING programs that make a difference “Across the Street” and “Around the World!" Our H.O.P.E. Programs provide help, both spiritually and physically, to those in need, such as the hungry, the homeless, the orphans, as well as those impacted by disasters; locally, nationally, and abroad. We don’t just send supplies, we are the “hands and feet of Jesus” on the ground to directly impact the lives of those in need. You too can be the “hands and feet of Jesus” by participating in one of our programs
